I live in New Zealand with my parents and my little brother, who honestly just loves to annoy me! My mum moved to New Zealand when I was about a year old, leaving me in India with my grandparents and uncle. A month later, my dad came too, so I was left alone for a bit and didn’t really understand what was happening.

I really missed my mum, even one day without her felt super hard. After six months, I finally moved to New Zealand to be with my family. We first lived in Manurewa, where I went to primary school. My brother was born in 2017, and then in 2019 we moved to our current place, where I continued school.
