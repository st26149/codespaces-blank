My Interests – Bhangra

One of my favourite things is Bhangra. I’ve loved it since I was little because it makes me happy, helps me forget stress, and keeps me in a good mood. It also helps me feel connected to my culture and where I come from.

Something I’m really proud of is getting into a well-known Bhangra academy and making it into the senior team. Because of that, I got to improve my skills, socialise with new people, perform with others, and had the chance to perform at concerts with Punjabi singers, which was super exciting.
